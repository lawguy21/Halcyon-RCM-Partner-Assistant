What You Need to Know (Plain Language Summary)

What is Financial Assistance?

Financial assistance is help paying for your hospital bills. Depending on your income and family size, you may qualify for:

  • Free Care: Your entire bill may be covered at no cost to you
  • Discounted Care: You may pay a reduced amount based on what you can afford
  • Payment Plans: We can set up affordable monthly payments

Who Can Apply?

Anyone can apply for financial assistance. You may qualify if:

  • You are uninsured or underinsured
  • Your income is below a certain level based on federal poverty guidelines
  • You have high medical bills compared to your income
  • You are experiencing financial hardship

How to Apply:

  • Complete our Financial Assistance Application
  • Provide proof of income (pay stubs, tax returns, benefit statements)
  • Submit your application to our Patient Financial Services department

What Happens After You Apply?

  1. We will review your application within 30 days

  • You will not be reported to credit bureaus
  • Interest and late fees will be paused
